## This is a post about my Odd Menu coding assignment!

For this assignment, we were practicing nested if...else statements. So, we had to make a program in C that would, based on the input of both either an odd number (secret menu) or even number (normal menu) and a number between 1 and 3 lead to a specific meal. 
I decided that my normal menu would be American Diner themed and my secret menu would be fancy french food.
